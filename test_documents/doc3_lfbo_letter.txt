Supervisory Assessment

Capital Planning & Positions remain broadly meets expectations.
The Tier 1 leverage ratio is 8.7%.
The total risk-based capital ratio is 11.9%.

Liquidity Risk Management is conditionally meets expectations.
The loan-to-deposit ratio is 86.1%.
Stress testing discussions highlighted depositor behavior sensitivities and contingency funding assumptions requiring continued refinement.

Governance & Controls are conditionally meets expectations, reflecting the linkage between risk management, earnings, and asset quality.
Nonperforming assets measure 2.0% of total assets, within peer tolerances;
return on assets of 1.18% supports capital generation.

Supervisory Expectations

The following actions are required to address outstanding supervisory concerns:
• Submit a detailed plan addressing Weaknesses in Contingency Funding Plan within 45 days.
• Provide a detailed plan addressing BSA/AML Compliance Deficiencies within 90 days.
• Provide a detailed plan addressing Inadequate Loan Review Function within 90 days.

• Provide quarterly updates describing progress against capital planning milestones and liquidity monetization triggers.

The Federal Reserve will monitor remediation through ongoing supervision and targeted work. Please engage your Dedicated Supervisory
Team Lead if clarification is required.
